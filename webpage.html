Supplemental Methods: Computation of the PA-QoL Score

The PA-QoL score is a standardized behavioral functioning index derived from eight routinely reported lifestyle components. The scoring procedure consists of four steps: (1) raw input extraction, (2) conversion of categorical responses to numerical values, (3) z-standardization using the derivation cohort's means and standard deviations (Supplemental Table 6), and (4) computation of a composite score rescaled to a T-score (mean = 50, SD = 10).
